# Privacy Policy for Silence Hero

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

Silence Hero respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our browser extension.

## 1. Data Collection
Silence Hero **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** or personally identifiable information (PII). 

## 2. Local Storage
The extension uses the `chrome.storage.local` API exclusively to save your preferences, such as:
- Notification settings
- Sound alert preferences
- Theme settings

This data stays locally on your device and is never uploaded to any remote server.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Silence Hero does not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or external APIs that collect user data.

## 4. Permissions
- **alarms**: Used to trigger logic updates for quiet hours transitions.
- **storage**: Used to store your local preferences.
- **notifications**: Used to show alerts when quiet hours begin or end.
- **offscreen**: Used for audio playback (silent/alerts) in compliance with Manifest V3.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/Andrewfragkiadakis/Silence-Hero
