About Quiet Hours

Greek quiet hours are regulated by Police Order 3 of 1996:

  • Summer (Apr-Sep): 15:00-17:30 & 23:00-07:00
  • Winter (Oct-Mar): 15:30-17:30 & 22:00-07:30

During these hours, noise should be kept to a minimum to respect neighbors and comply with local regulations.
